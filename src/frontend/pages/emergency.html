About Emergency System
AccelerateZero Emergency Response v2.0

This system uses device sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, GPS) to detect possible accidents and automatically trigger emergency workflows. In offline mode, emergency data is stored locally and synced when connectivity is restored.

All emergency data is encrypted and securely transmitted. Your location is only shared during active emergencies.
